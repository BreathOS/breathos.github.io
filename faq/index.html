What is Breath OS?

Breath OS is the first operating system that can adapt to your computer to the tasks that you perform regularly by one click. Handy, isn’t it?

Why do I need it?

For saving your time, of course! Imagine, you have bought a new computer. How much time will you spend to setting it up for daily usage? Could you do it without the helping hand of a computer master?

OMG! How do you do this?

Statistics and programming, nothing more. We just did research in the behavior of computer users and realized that sometimes people who are involved in the same specialty or hobby use the same stack of programs and even configurations for them. So, we decided to collect thematic programs and configuration into suitcases.

And... What is a suitcase?

Just a package that contains all necessary data to configure your PC. For example, a suitcase called "A JS dev’s pack" probably will store some programs and configurations for comfortable development on the JavaScript programming language. You can also create a new one and share with others: just create your own suitcase via built-in Bi creator app and contact with us.

So, what should I do to use Breath OS?

Actually, nothing. Just create a bootable medium with us .iso file and install it like Ubuntu or Linux Mint. Then open BStore and chose suitcase suitable for you.

Is it free!?

Absolutely! But you can still support us with a piece of advice or money. Just contact with us via e-mail.
